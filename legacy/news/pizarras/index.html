In October 2012 I held an exhibition in Taiwan called On The Way To...

Here's a video to see how it looked.

These are some archived news from that lovely time in Taiwan:

  • I met Charles Landry author the book The Creative City: A Toolkit for Urban Innovators An article about the meeting can be read on the webpage of URSTaipei, the project he's running in Taiwan.
  • I'm currently in Taiwan working with the people of CAMPOBAG project. CAMPOBAG provides a platform for young artists to develop their creative brands and help them to manage their creations. The word CAMPO is an acronym for Cinema, Art, Music, People, and Original. The project I've been invited to is Cooking Together. The idea behind Cooking Together is to facilitate people an active space where they can motivate each other and have a connection between their works.
