Leaves

These leaves come from the parks and trails, all are important and unique, that once stood here like us and every spring they return, the figures are made with simple pieces of wood coming from cuts in carpentry.

Is an artistic expression that reflects us in our daily lives, whatever we do, there are many times when we feel well, like a leaf in the wind ...
